Chess Rules - How to Play Chess



Chess is one of the most popular games in the world. Here are the basic steps to get started:

  • Set up the chess board correctly
  • Learn how each piece moves
  • Understand the special rules (castling, en passant, promotion)
  • Decide who makes the first move
  • Learn the rules on how to win (checkmate and stalemate)
  • Practice basic strategies for beginners
  • Play games to improve your skills


You can also practice chess against computers online. This is a great way to learn while playing:

  • iChess: Play at various difficulty levels from casual to Grandmaster
  • Chess-Computer.com: Instant play with adjustable skill levels
  • 365Chess.com: Challenge Stockfish 17 at different strengths
  • Chess.com: Play against computer opponents with coach bots
  • CardGames.io: Free online play without needing an account
