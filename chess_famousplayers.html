Famous Chess Players

Chess has been shaped by legendary players throughout history. Studying their games helps players of all levels improve strategy, tactics, and understanding of competitive chess.

  • Garry Kasparov: World Champion 1985-2000, known for aggressive and dynamic play
  • Bobby Fischer: American Grandmaster, World Champion 1972, famous for match against Spassky
  • Magnus Carlsen: Modern World Champion, known for positional mastery and endgame skills
  • Anatoly Karpov: World Champion 1975-1985, celebrated for strategic precision
  • José Raúl Capablanca: Cuban World Champion 1921-1927, renowned for intuitive play and endgame mastery
  • Vishwanathan Anand: Indian Grandmaster, World Champion 2007-2013, known for rapid calculation and versatility
